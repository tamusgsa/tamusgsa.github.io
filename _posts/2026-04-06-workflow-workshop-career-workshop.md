### Description

We've invited alumni and current students working across academia, pharma, finance, and AI/ML - based on your survey responses. 

Given how challenging the current job market is, this is a chance to hear what actually worked for people who recently went through the process and to ask questions you haven't been able to answer on your own. 
For the first and second year students - we will be asking their advice on early stage strategy, so please come prepared with questions! 
For the third and fourth year students - many of the panelists have completed internships, so this is also a great opportunity to ask internship-related questions. 
We will also have dedicated time for international students to discuss job search strategy.
